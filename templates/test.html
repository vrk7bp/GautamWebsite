About Me

A bit more insight into the man behind the mask.




"My best friend is the one who brings out the best in me."

So you want to learn more about me, huh? Who can blame you, I'm a pretty interesting person. As you have probably figured out by now, I am a 4th Year student at the University of Virginia studying Computer Engineering and Economics (with an Engineering Business Minor). I am a pretty big sports fan, and love supporting the Virginia Cavaliers in every sport we play. I'm especially passionate about our basketball team, and dream about the day we win a National Championship.

From a programming standpoint, I am really interested in Cloud Computing and have recently dived into learning about Machine Learning. Although building this website has been a worthwhile project, and I've learned a lot from it, I definitely lean more toward back-end development as oppossed to front-end. My passion and pursuit to become a great programmer really started less than a year ago, in the second semester of my third year, and since then I have been coding and learning non-stop. Some of my bigger coding projects have been creating a more rudimentary version of DropBox , an Android Application for a self-guided tour through UVa , and a geography toy that integrated RFID and push-button sensors along with a GUI and question generator (my first major coding project from my first semester first year).
